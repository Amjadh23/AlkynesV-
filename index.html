Understanding ASD

The Problems

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a complex developmental condition involving persistent challenges with social communication, restricted interests and repetitive behavior.

Recognizing Expressions

Difficulty recognizing facial expressions, body language and emotional cues — making it hard to understand what others are feeling.

Social Interactions

Responding appropriately in social interactions can be overwhelming, leading to anxiety and avoidance of social situations.

Limited Safe Practice

Limited opportunities to practice social skills safely without risk of embarrassment or negative consequences.

Our Answer

The Solution

A VR platform that trains autistic learners to produce the right facial expressions using real-time AI feedback.

By leveraging Meta Quest 3 and integrating intelligent AI-driven dialogue, ALKYNESV has the potential to enhance social communication skills and further support users' ability to interact more confidently in everyday life.

The Process

How It Works

01

User Wears VR Headset

Meta Quest 3 provides an immersive, safe environment for social training scenarios.

02

AI Tracks Facial Movements

Convai: AI Driven Dialogue and Personality using gemini-2.5-flash analyzes and interprets user expressions in real time.

03

System Compares Expression

UI/UX Framework for Guiding Users in Accurate Facial Expression Selection evaluates user performance.

04

Real-Time Feedback

Instant, gentle feedback guides users to improve their facial expressions and emotional responses.

05

Social Scenario Practice

Practice interacting with AI-driven avatars in realistic social scenarios to build confidence.
